# RISK ALERT SYSTEM

## Risk Indicators
- Sudden market downturn
- Increased geopolitical tensions
- Regulatory changes in key markets
- Significant changes in customer behavior

## Alert Rules
1. **Market Downturn**: Alert if market indexes drop by more than 5% within a single day.
2. **Geopolitical Tension**: Alert if there are breaking news updates about conflict or unrest in key markets.
3. **Regulatory Changes**: Alert if new legislation is proposed that could impact operations.
4. **Customer Behavior**: Alert if there is a 20% drop in user engagement week-over-week.

## Escalation Procedures
1. If an alert is triggered, notify the risk management team immediately via email.
2. The risk management team will assess the situation and provide an evaluation within 24 hours.
3. Based on the evaluation, further actions may include a strategy meeting to discuss potential impacts and responses.
